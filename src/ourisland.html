Taniti

Taniti is a small island with a big heart. Whether you come by air or by sea, we welcome all who want to enjoy our safe and happy island. Taniti, your home away from home.

Frequently Asked Questions:

Will I be able to charge my electronics?

Power outlets are 120 volts, which is the same as in the United States of America.

What are drinking laws in Taniti?

The drinking age in Taniti is 18, however age is a relative concept. Alcohol cannot be served or sold between the hours of midnight and 9:00 a.m.

I only speak my native language, will it be difficult to travel here because of that?

Many younger Tanitians speak fluent English, however in rural areas and especially with older residents, English is not as well known. In case of emergencies, there are many multilingual employees at the hospital and clinics.

This is my first time traveling, I'm nervous to be in a new place.

Taniti is a very safe island, violent crime is very rare so you can feel comfortable enough to make this your home.

What currency is used in Taniti?

Taniti uses the U.S. dollar but many businesses will also accept euros and yen. Several banks facilitate currency exchange and many businesses accept major credit cards.
